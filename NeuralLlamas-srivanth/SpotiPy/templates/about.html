About Us

Focus Flow is a music recommendation platform designed to help users focus and relax. By connecting with Spotify, users can access playlists curated for concentration or relaxation. The project integrates EEG data analysis to adaptively recommend music based on brainwave responses, creating a personalized experience to enhance productivity and reduce stress.
